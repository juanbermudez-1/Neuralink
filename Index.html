About Neuralink

Neuralink is a neurotechnology company founded by Elon Musk with the goal of creating a brain-machine interface (BMI). It aims to develop ultra-high bandwidth devices to connect the human brain to computers.

Technology

Neuralink's core technology involves implanting small, flexible threads into the brain using a custom robotic system. These threads can record and stimulate neural activity, enabling a direct link between the brain and external devices.

Applications

  • Helping people with neurological conditions such as paralysis or Parkinson's disease.
  • Enhancing human cognition and memory capabilities.
  • Creating seamless interfaces for controlling devices with thought.
